A practical resource for doing digital community engagement

This playbook features reusable and transferable case studies of digital community engagement that you can learn from and apply in your own work.

More about community engagement

By community engagement, we mean interacting with various groups and individuals within a local area - to understand their views, gather their ideas, start a dialogue or help them take action.

The case studies in this playbook aim to go beyond just informing people; they are about engaging in a meaningful way. Doing this helps to build trust and ensure that local authorities are responding well to the real needs of their communities.

Some case studies will be relevant for statutory consultation, but the playbook’s main focus is engagement more broadly.
